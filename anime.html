The anime adaptation started airing in 1999. ### Story Overview *One Piece* is set in a fantastical world where pirates roam the seas in search of the ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece," which is said to grant its possessor the title of the Pirate King. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young and energetic pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy's dream is to find the One Piece and become the Pirate King. ### Main Characters - **Monkey D. Luffy**: The main protagonist, Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. The series explores themes such as freedom, adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams. It also delves into deeper issues like oppression, racism, and the morality of government and power. ### Popularity and Impact *One Piece* has gained immense popularity worldwide, with a large and dedicated fanbase. The series is ongoing, with both the manga and anime continuing to release new content, making it a long-running and influential part of the global pop culture landscape.
